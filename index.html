  • Reminder: For those on Smartpoint during the shift: You are not on an island alone, if you find that you are backed up ask for help. We are a team and if we load balance it will effectively be less work and less errors made. Never be afraid to ask questions or ask for help. Same goes for carbon peeps.
  • When engaging another company to assist us with a customer issue. [carrier issues, vendors, etc] Please type all relevant numbers/phone numbers in internal comments. This helps speed the process for when the ticket gets handed off and has saved my butt on multiple occasions.
  • When triaging alarms, especially registration and network connectivity errors, a critical eye needs to be placed upon the system to see if there are any other issues present.
    • Example: A media-gateway OOS alarm comes into Carbon, you access the system and see that it has self-resolved. Look at the other media-gateways to ensure that nothing else was effected by the bounce. Are there other alarms that came in at the same time that haven’t resolved?
    • Remember: Just because an alarm resolved, does not always mean the issue itself has gone away. Look to see if the alarm is recurring or not. This is an indicator that something much larger is going on.
